Lesson 3: Numbers in Baghdad Arabic

"Learn how to count!"

Numbers 1–10

  • Wahid (Wa-heed): One
  • Ithnayn (Ith-nayn): Two
  • Thalatha (Tha-la-tha): Three
  • Arba'a (Ar-ba-a): Four
  • Khamsa (Kham-sa): Five
  • Sitta (Sit-ta): Six
  • Sab'a (Sab-aa): Seven
  • Thamaniya (Tha-ma-nee-ya): Eight
  • Tis'a (Tis-aa): Nine
  • Ashara (Ash-ra): Ten
