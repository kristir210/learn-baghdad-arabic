Everyday Phrases

Learn useful everyday phrases in Baghdad Arabic:

  • Thank you: Shukran
  • Please: Minfadlak
  • Excuse me: Afwan
  • Sorry: Asif
  • Yes: Aiwa
  • No: La
  • How much is this?: Besh hal?
  • I don’t understand: Ma fahimt
  • Can you repeat that?: Tislam tkrar?
  • I’m hungry: Ana ju’aan
  • I’m thirsty: Ana athshan
  • Where is the bathroom?: Wayn il-hammam?
  • What is this?: Shino hada?
  • I need help: Ana biddi musa’ada
  • It’s okay: Tamam
  • Very good: Kteer mnih
  • I don’t know: Ma a’rif
  • Let’s go: Yallah nroo7
  • See you later: Yallah nshufak baad
