Lesson: Greetings

Learn common Baghdad Arabic greetings:

  • Hello: Marhaba
  • Good morning: Sabah al-noor
  • Good evening: Masa al-jamal
  • How are you?: Keefak?
  • Welcome: Ahlan bik
  • Good night: Tisbah bikhair
  • See you later: Nshufak baadan
  • Goodbye: Fi aman Allah
  • What’s up?: Shlon halak?
  • Pleased to meet you: Mutasharraf
  • How’s your day?: Kayf yomak?
  • I’m doing well: Ana bikhair, shukran
  • Take care of yourself: Khod care nafsak
  • Have a safe journey: Safar sa’eed
  • May God bless you: Allah ybarik fik
  • Peace be upon you: Assalamu alaykum
  • And upon you peace: Wa alaykum assalam
  • Long time no see: Min zamman ma shufnak
  • How have you been?: Kayf kunak?
  • Wishing you all the best: Kol el tawaafuk
