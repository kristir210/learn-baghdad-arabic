Lesson 2: Common Phrases in Baghdad Arabic

"Speak like a local!"

Common Phrases

  • Shlonek? (Sh-lo-nek): How are you? (to a male)
  • Shlonich? (Sh-lo-neech): How are you? (to a female)
  • Kayf al-hal? (Kai-f al-hal): How’s it going?
  • Alhamdulillah (Al-hamd-u-lil-lah): Thanks be to God
  • Tashrif? (Tash-rif): Would you like to come in?
  • Shukran (Shook-ran): Thank you
  • Min fadlak (Min fa-dlak): Please (to a male)
  • Min fadlich (Min fa-dleech): Please (to a female)
