Lesson: Common Phrases

Learn common Baghdad Arabic phrases:

  • Hello: ahlan
  • Good morning: sabah al-khair
  • Good evening: masa al-khair
  • How are you?: shlonek
  • Welcome: ahlan wa sahlan
  • Good night: tisbah ala khair
  • See you later: yalla nshufak baad
  • Goodbye: maa al-salama
  • What’s up?: shlonek
  • Thank you: shukran
  • Please: minfadlak
  • Excuse me: afuwan
  • What is your name?: shismek
  • My name is...: ismi
  • Nice to meet you: mtsharfin
  • How’s it going?: keef halak
  • I’m fine: ana bikhair
  • Take care: khod care
  • Have a good day: yom saeed
  • Congratulations: mabrook
